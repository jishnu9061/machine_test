ABOUT US

Destiny is not matter of chance. It is a matter of choice. It is not a thing to be waited for; it is a thing to be achieved. At International Placewell Consultants, Gulf we believe in this talisman. When a student is standing at the threshold of his future, deciding the educational route, the decisions that he/she takes shapes their destiny.

We at IPC endeavour to hold your hand as you take the most crucial step towards your goals.International Placewell Consultants, Gulf an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, is one of the primary education consultants and approved academic counsellor in the U.A.E. IPC, Gulf is licensed by the U.A.E Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research to represent leading Universities and Colleges worldwide.Since its inception, in 2003 we have been based at Knowledge Village, Dubai and have placed hundreds of students to various universities and colleges worldwide.

We provide complete counselling for students who wish to pursue their higher education overseas. Our team of consultants possess comprehensive understanding of the academic institutions, eligibility factor and current developments. Personal and financial matters relevant to both parents and students are given complete importance and taken into account before carving the due path.We act as a bridge between the university and the student, wherein we provide career counselling, visa assistance, admissions as well as pre-departure briefing to students to ensure that the students find the entire process hassle free.
